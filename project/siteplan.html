Starlight Echo

Quick facts and visuals about planets, stars, and galaxies — perfect for curious minds exploring the universe.

Site Name

Starlight Echo

The name Starlight Echo captures the spirit of discovery and fascination with the vastness of space. It’s short, memorable, and conveys both science and awe.

Site Purpose

This website aims to educate and inspire users with great visuals and fascinating facts about our universe. It features an introduction to how incredible the universe is, an overview of the solar system’s planets, and a gallery of mind-blowing facts about the universe.

Scenarios

  • How many planets are in our solar system and what makes each unique?
  • What are some of the most incredible and little-known facts about the universe?

Color Schema

#1E1E2F – main background and header/footer.
#AB82FF – for headings and highlight accents.
#00F5FF – for links and hover effects.

Typography

Orbitron – used for headings and the site title to convey a futuristic, space-themed feel.

Rajdhani – used for paragraph text and descriptions for easy readability.
